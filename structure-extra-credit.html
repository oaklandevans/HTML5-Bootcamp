myPlate

MyPlate is a reminder to find your healthy eating style and build it throughout your lifetime. Everthing you eat and drink matters. The right mix can help you be healthier now and in the future. This means:

  • Focus on variety, amount, and nutrition.
  • Choose foods and beverages with less saturated fat, sodium, and added sugars.
  • Start with small changes to build bealthier eating styles.
  • Support healthy eating for everyone.
